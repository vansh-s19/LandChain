# How LandChain Works (Step-by-Step)

LandChain makes the complex process of registering and transferring property very simple. Here is how it works under the hood, explained simply:

### 1. Smart Scanning
When you upload your paper property document to the website, our system acts like a hyper-intelligent reader. Using a technology called **OCR (Optical Character Recognition)**, it reads the image of the document and automatically pulls out important details like your Name, Plot Number, and Area. You don't need to manually type everything out!

### 2. Unbreakable Storage
Once your document is scanned, where do we save the actual file? Not on a normal computer server where it could be hacked or deleted. We save it on **IPFS (InterPlanetary File System)**. 
Think of IPFS as a global web of computers that chops your file into tiny pieces and scatters them across the world. It gives you a unique mathematical "fingerprint" for your file. If anyone tries to change even a single pixel of your document, the fingerprint changes completely, and the system instantly knows it's a fake.

### 3. The Indestructible Record
Finally, we take that unique fingerprint and save it to the **Blockchain** (the shared digital notebook we talked about earlier). We link this fingerprint to your personal "Digital Wallet" (which acts like your digital ID card).
Now, the blockchain permanently says: *"The person holding this digital wallet ID is the true owner of the document with this exact fingerprint."* 

### 4. Government Verification
Just because you uploaded a document doesn't mean it's officially yours yet. A government authority logs into the system, checks the scanned document, and clicks "Verify". Once verified, the whole world knows the government has legally approved your ownership, and this approval is cemented into the blockchain forever.

### 5. Transferring Ownership
Want to sell your property? In the old days, this meant stacks of paperwork and weeks of waiting. On LandChain, you simply initiate a transfer to the buyer's digital wallet. A "Smart Contract" (a digital robot that follows strict rules) automatically updates the blockchain. Instantly, the digital notebook crosses out your name and writes the buyer's name. It's as fast and easy as sending an email!
