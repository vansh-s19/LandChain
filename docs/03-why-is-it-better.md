# Why is LandChain Better?

You might wonder, "Why go through all this trouble with Blockchain and Smart Scanners? Why not just use a normal government database?"

Here is why LandChain is a massive upgrade over the traditional property system:

### 1. It's Tamper-Proof (No More Fraud)
In a traditional database, a corrupt official or a computer hacker could sneak in and change the ownership record from "Alice's House" to "Bob's House". 
On the blockchain, changing a record requires hacking thousands of computers simultaneously. It's practically impossible. Your property ownership is locked in by complex mathematics, making land theft a thing of the past.

### 2. Transparent History
Have you ever wondered who owned a house 50 years ago, and if there are any hidden legal disputes? 
LandChain keeps a permanent, un-erasable history of every owner, ever. Anyone can look up a property and trace its lineage all the way back to the day it was built. No more hidden surprises when buying a home.

### 3. Lightning Fast Transfers
Transferring property usually requires lawyers, banks, notaries, and government clerks, taking weeks or even months. 
Because LandChain uses "Smart Contracts" (computer programs that automatically execute the rules without needing human middlemen), a property transfer can happen in seconds once both the buyer and seller agree.

### 4. No Lost Documents
Because your original property deed is stored on the decentralized IPFS network, it can never be lost in a fire, destroyed in a flood, or accidentally deleted from a broken server. It exists permanently and safely distributed across the internet.
